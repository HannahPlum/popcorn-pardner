What's in the Saddlebag

Everything you need to track and share the movies that matter to you.

Movie Search

Search millions of films powered by the TMDB API. Find anything from a 1940s western to last weekend's blockbuster.

Watchlist

Round up movies you haven't seen yet and keep them in your personal watchlist so nothing slips through the fence.

Movie Reviews

Log any film you've watched and leave a written review posted to your profile for friends and followers to read.

Hat Ratings

Rate every movie 1–5 cowboy hats. Because stars are for ordinary folks, and you're no ordinary pardner.

Follow Friends

Follow other users and see their latest logs, reviews, and ratings right in your dedicated friends' feed.

Comments

Spark a conversation. Comment on your own reviews or reply to what your friends are watching and thinking.

Currently Watching

Let the trail know you're mid-film. A live indicator on your profile shows friends exactly what you're watching right now.

Built With

A lean, frontend-first stack — no backend required.

TMDB API Movie data & metadata
